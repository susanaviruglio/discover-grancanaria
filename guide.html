Transports

Taxi

There are different taxi companies you can use for short distances within the island. All taxis have meters and regulated fares.

Approximate fares from the airport:
Destination Price (€)
Las Palmas €27 – €30
Maspalomas €40 – €50
Puerto Rico €55 – €65
Puerto de Mogán €60 – €70

Bus

Using the bus in Gran Canaria is a great option if you don’t want to spend too much on a taxi from the airport. The island has a reliable and affordable public transport system with two main companies. Global operates interurban routes, connecting the airport with towns and tourist areas across the island, making it ideal for longer distances. In Las Palmas, Guaguas Municipales runs the urban network — these are the distinctive yellow buses that cover the city efficiently.

Maps

Using a map is essential when exploring Gran Canaria. The island has many different areas, from busy cities like Las Palmas to tourist destinations in the south such as Maspalomas and Puerto Rico.

You can also use digital maps like Google Maps to plan routes, check bus connections, and find attractions easily.
